About Iron Wolf

A repair-first garage door company built around clarity.

Iron Wolf Garage Door is set up for homeowners who want the issue explained plainly, the price approved before the work begins, and the warranty reviewed before the truck leaves. The goal is not to create pressure. The goal is to solve the problem correctly and document what was done.

How visits work

What a homeowner should expect from the appointment.

Diagnosis first

We inspect balance, spring tension, tracks, rollers, hinges, cables, safety sensors, and opener behavior before recommending parts.

Photos before parts

When a part has failed, the problem is shown and explained before the repair is approved.

Written estimate

The number is reviewed before work starts, including labor and the parts being installed.

Test cycle together

When the job is done, the door is cycled, the work is reviewed, and the warranty is handed over in writing.

Quote and payment policy

The small details that usually create friction are handled up front.

New door estimates are free. Repair appointments may include a diagnostic fee, and that fee is credited toward approved work when the repair moves forward. Cash, check, and major credit cards are accepted.

Free install estimates

Replacement-door consultations are quoted without a fee so you can compare door styles, insulation, and hardware calmly.

Credited diagnostic work

Repair diagnostics are meant to lead to a real answer, not to trap you in a second conversation about price.

Warranty follow-through

Repairs and installations are backed by written parts-and-labor coverage that is reviewed before the job is closed out.

Reach out

Questions first, appointment second is fine too.

If you want to talk through the repair, the quote process, or whether a replacement is even necessary, call or send the form and we will walk through it with you.
