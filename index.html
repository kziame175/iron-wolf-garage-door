FAQ

Answers before you call.

How much does garage door spring replacement cost?

It depends on spring size, door weight, and hardware condition. We quote the full price before work begins and explain why the part is needed.

Do you offer same-day service?

Most repair calls can be handled the same day, especially broken springs, stuck doors, opener issues, and doors that will not close securely.

Is there a charge for an estimate?

New door quotes are free. Repair visits may include a diagnostic fee that is credited toward the repair when you approve the work.

Can you repair my opener, or do I need a new one?

We repair sensors, remotes, keypads, travel limits, belts, chains, and worn components. If replacement is smarter, we will tell you why.

What brands do you service?

We service common garage door and opener brands including LiftMaster, Chamberlain, Genie, Clopay, Amarr, and Wayne Dalton.

Do you warranty your work?

Yes. Repairs and installations include a written warranty on parts and labor.
