Policy details

What is collected and why.

What information is collected?

Name, phone number, email address, service address when provided, and job details submitted through calls, email, or website forms.

How is that information used?

To respond to quote requests, schedule service, communicate about an appointment, and document the work requested or completed.

Is information sold to third parties?

No. Information submitted for service requests is not sold. It may be shared only with vendors needed to operate the website or process communications.

How can you request changes?

Email hello@ironwolfgaragedoor.com or call 630-703-0039 if you want your contact information updated or removed from follow-up records.
