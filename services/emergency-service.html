Common symptoms

What we see on emergency service calls.

Door stuck open overnight

Broken spring plus dead disconnect — we stabilize, secure, and repair before nightfall.

Car trapped inside

Manual release, cable inspection, and emergency spring swap so you can get out the same day.

Door came off the tracks

Immediate stabilization, then rebuild of tracks and rollers with correct spacing.

Cable snapped mid-cycle

Both cables and often springs replaced together to restore safe balance.

Sensor failure in a storm

Photo-eye alignment, wiring check, and opener logic test in a single visit.

Bumper impact

Panel-only or section-only repair keeps the door running while custom panels come in.

FAQ

Emergency Service questions we hear.

Do you charge extra for emergency service?

Standard rates during posted hours (Mon–Sat 7am–7pm). After-hours dispatch carries an after-hours fee that we quote before we roll.

How fast can you get here?

In Naperville proper: usually 30–60 minutes for true emergencies. Adjacent suburbs: 45–90 minutes.

Can you get my car out?

Yes, in nearly every case. Manual release plus a rope-and-clamp stabilization gets the door up long enough to move the vehicle.

Is a stuck-open door a security risk?

Yes. We can lock the door in the closed position with clamps as a same-visit stopgap while parts arrive.
